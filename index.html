PFC

My collection of homemade CPUs


PFC stands for...

  • Perfectly Fine CPU
  • Probably Functional CPU
  • PFC is a Functional Computer

... and whatnots.


PFC is a series of 8-bit handmade CPUs to be made out of discrete logic components, like 7400 series integrated circuits. There are two versions of this CPUs: PFC mini and PFC full.


PFC mini

Status: gonna happen |  ISA & Emulator: here (unfinished!)

PFC mini is a Harvard architecture 8-bit computer with 256 bytes each of program and data memory. It is capable of running basic programs, such as calculating Fibonacci numbers, sieving primes, and playing Snake. It will be built on breadboard with 74HCxx ICs.


PFC full

Status: might happen |  ISA & Emulator: N/A

PFC full is a von Neumann architecture 8-bit computer with at least 65 kB of memory. It is planned to be somewhat on par with 8080, have a VGA output, and a custom sound card.
